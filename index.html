About Us

This page is designed to make enjoying music a little easier. If you want to find a concert, you can use this site to look for local concerts by putting in your zip code. Once you have a list of nearby shows, you can put the band names into the other search bar to find videos of the groups. We have also included a feature to save search results in order for you to see what performers are popular. We hope this site helps you find a great show!
